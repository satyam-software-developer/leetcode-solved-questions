# [Print Square wall](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/print-square-wall-1605682270--111211/1?page=3&category=CPP&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
Given an integer&nbsp;n,&nbsp;&nbsp;write a program to print the square wall of size&nbsp;n&nbsp;using a single loop and string multiplication.&nbsp;
Examples:
Input: n = 5
Output:
* * * * ** * * * ** * * * ** * * * ** * * * *
Explanation: Its perfect square wall. 
Input: n = 4Output:* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * Explanation: Its perfect square wall.
