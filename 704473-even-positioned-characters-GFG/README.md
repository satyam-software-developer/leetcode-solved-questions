# [Even Positioned Characters](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/for-loop-2/1?page=1&category=CPP&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
You are given a String S, you need to print its characters at even indices(index starts at 0). 
Examples: 
Input: s = "Geeks"
Output: Ges
Explanation: The even indices (0, 2 &amp; 4) characters are printed.
Input: s = "DoctorPhenomenal"
Output: DcoPeoea
Explanation: The even indices characters are printed.
