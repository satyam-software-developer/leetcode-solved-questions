# [C++ Multi-level inheritance](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/c-multi-level-inheritance/1?page=1&category=CPP&status=unsolved&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
Ankit is a very competitive person and always tries to compare him to other. He has got 5 subjects in his course and he wants to make a list of total marks and average marks of the students in his class with their roll numbers. He wants to use the concept of multi-level inheritance doing this. Help him achieve the required goal.&nbsp; &nbsp;
Create 2 classes:

Test: containing the marks of a student in 5 subjects inheriting class&nbsp;student ( having roll number of the student).
Result: containing the function Display() to compute the total and average and then displaying the output as Roll number, total and average which are space separated.

Note:- Student class is already been created and has functions to set roll number.
Examples :
Input: 10 10 10 10 10Output: 1 50 10
Input: 8 8 8 8 8 Output: 2 40 8
Input: 7 7 7 7 7 Output: 3 35 7
Constraints:&nbsp;1 &lt; n &lt; 1000 &lt;= marks &lt;= 100
